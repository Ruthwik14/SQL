# Balanced Tree Clothing Company Sales Analysis

## Project Description
The Balanced Tree Clothing Company Sales Analysis project aims to provide comprehensive insights into the sales data of Balanced Tree, a fictional clothing company. By leveraging SQL queries, we analyzed various aspects of sales transactions, product performance, and customer behavior to inform strategic business decisions and optimize operational efficiency.

## Database Structure
The project utilizes a relational database management system structured into four main tables:
1. **Product hierarchy:** Stores hierarchical information about product categories, segments, and styles.
2. **Product prices:** Contains pricing information for different products.
3. **Product details:** Provides detailed information about products including names, categories, segments, and styles.
4. **Sales:** Captures transactional data including product IDs, quantities, prices, discounts, membership status, transaction IDs, and timestamps.

## Solution
### Part A: High-Level Sales Analysis
We conducted high-level sales analysis to understand overall sales performance:
- Determined the total quantity sold for all products.
- Calculated the total generated revenue before discounts.
- Analyzed the total discount amount for all products.

### Part B: Transaction Analysis
We delved into transactional data to gain insights into customer behavior:
- Identified the number of unique transactions.
- Calculated the average number of unique items purchased per transaction.
- Analyzed the average discount value per transaction.
- Examined the percentage split of transactions for members vs. non-members.

### Part C: Product Analysis
We explored product performance across different categories and segments:
- Identified the top-selling products by total revenue before discount.
- Determined the top-selling product for each segment and category.
- Analyzed the percentage split of total revenue by category and segment.
- Investigated the total transaction "penetration" for each product.

## Conclusion
Through this analysis, we gained valuable insights into Balanced Tree Clothing Company's sales dynamics. We identified top-performing products, analyzed customer behavior, and understood the impact of discounts on revenue. These insights can guide strategic decisions, such as optimizing product offerings, targeting specific customer segments, and refining pricing strategies to maximize profitability and enhance customer satisfaction.

By leveraging data-driven approaches, Balanced Tree Clothing Company can refine its business strategies, improve operational efficiency, and drive sustainable growth in the competitive fashion market.
